Washington, DC Area's Modern, Mid-Century &
Contemporary Real Estate Source.

DCModernHomes.com is dedicated to the principle of home as expression, with a concentration on architectural, historic and luxury real estate properties. The site is curated by Andre Perez, founder of District Partners at Compass and a leading proponent of modern and contemporary real estate in the Washington area.

Our mission is to provide a consummate real estate resource. Visitors will find easy access to property listings, neighborhood profiles, buyer and seller resources, home styles and other informative content.

With over 6.2 million residents, the Washington Metropolitan area is one of the most populous and fastest-growing in the nation. This vibrant region is home to Washington DC and the federal seat of government as well as Northern Virginia and parts of Maryland.

From grand estates to contemporary design, and from bustling urban corridors to quiet tree-lined suburbs, DC Modern Homes has you covered. We hope you enjoy this window into Washington’s exciting homes, condos and lifestyle experience.

About Andre Perez & Compass

Andre Perez leads the District Partners Real Estate team at Compass, the fastest growing brokerage in the nation. Our focus is on the Washington Metro region with offices in Alexandria, Virginia with additional offices in North Arlington, Washington, DC and Montgomery County, Maryland.  

Our strategic alliance with Compass affords us an incredible array of tools and technology while still offering concierge service to our clients. Compass is an American real estate technology company headquartered in New York City with a growing global network.

Washington’s status as the nation’s capital makes it a unique destination marketplace for home buyers who come from near and far. Andre Perez and his team of dedicated individuals combine local and international expertise, along with a deep appreciation for iconic architecture so emblematic of the region. From classic antiques to modern and contemporary design, the DC Metro area has a deep inventory of available properties for sale.

While the sheer number of homes, condos, townhouses, and neighborhoods in the area can seem daunting, our DC Modern Homes platform helps bring clarity and focus to the process. When you’re ready to move to the next step, Andre, his team and the entire Compass family are here to make your search a fruitful and seamless experience.
